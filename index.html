Things about all dogs, all dogs need sink water with no ice cubes. They also need food, make sure it is the right kind of food ( ask the vet what kind of food they need). They also need a comfy dog bed,leash,a dog brush,dog shampoo and conditioner make sure it is the right kind of shampoo and conditioner. If the dog has pale color fur you will need eye tear stain wipes.
